# TechMart Electronics — Warranty Policy

## Standard warranty
All TechMart-branded devices include a **1-year limited warranty** covering
manufacturing defects. Third-party products carry the manufacturer's warranty.

## Premium extended warranty
TechMart Premium members receive **+1 additional year** of warranty on eligible
devices (laptops, smartphones) at no extra cost, for a total of 2 years.

## What's covered
- Hardware defects and component failures under normal use.
- Battery capacity falling below 70% within the warranty period.

## What's not covered
- Accidental damage, liquid damage, and cracked screens (covered separately by
  TechMart Protect, sold as an add-on).
- Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications.

## Making a claim
Start a warranty claim from your account under "Orders" → "Get help" → "Warranty",
or email support@techmart.example with your order ID and a description of the
issue. Approved claims are repaired or replaced within 7–10 business days.
