# TechMart Electronics — General User Manual

## Managing your account
From **Account** you can update your profile, addresses, payment methods, and
membership. Changes save immediately.

## Orders
- View, track, and return orders under **Orders**.
- Start a return within the 30-day window using "Return item".
- Download invoices as PDF from any completed order.

## Membership benefits
TechMart Premium benefits apply automatically at checkout once active: free
express shipping, extended warranty, and priority queue for support.

## Notifications
Control order, shipping, and promotional notifications under
**Account → Notifications**. You can turn marketing emails off without affecting
order updates.

## Getting help
Use the in-app support assistant for instant answers. Complex or unresolved
issues are escalated to a human specialist, who will follow up by email.
