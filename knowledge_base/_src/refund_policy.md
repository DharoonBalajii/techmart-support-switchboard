# TechMart Electronics — Refund & Cancellation Policy

## Return window
Most items can be returned within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund,
provided they are in original condition with all accessories and packaging.

## Non-returnable items
Opened software, gift cards, and personalized/engraved items are non-returnable
unless they arrive damaged or defective.

## Refund timelines
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- Once we receive and inspect the return, refunds are processed within
  **5–7 business days**.
- Card refunds may take an additional 2–3 business days to appear on your
  statement depending on your bank.

## TechMart Premium refunds
Premium membership can be cancelled anytime. If you cancel within **14 days** of
purchase and have not used Premium benefits, you are eligible for a full refund.
After 14 days, Premium is refunded on a pro-rated basis for the unused period.

## Double charges & billing errors
If you were charged twice or billed in error, contact billing@techmart.example
with your order ID. Verified duplicate charges are refunded within
**3 business days**.

## Failed upgrades
If you paid for Premium but the benefits did not activate, this is treated as a
billing + technical issue. We will restore access immediately and, if it cannot
be restored, issue a full refund.
