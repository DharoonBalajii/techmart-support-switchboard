# TechMart Electronics — Shipping Policy

## Delivery options
- **Standard:** 3–5 business days. Free on orders over $49.
- **Express:** 2 business days. $12, or free for Premium members.
- **Same-day:** available in select metro areas for $19.

## Order tracking
A tracking link is emailed when your order ships. You can also track orders from
your account under "Orders".

## International shipping
We ship to 30+ countries. International delivery takes 7–14 business days; duties
and import taxes may apply and are the customer's responsibility.

## Delayed or lost packages
If tracking has not updated for 5+ business days, contact support and we will open
an investigation with the carrier. Confirmed lost packages are re-shipped or
refunded.
