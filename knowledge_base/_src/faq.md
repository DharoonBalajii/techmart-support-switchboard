# TechMart Electronics — Frequently Asked Questions

## About TechMart
TechMart Electronics is an online retailer of laptops, smartphones, smart-home
devices, and accessories. We also sell **TechMart Premium**, a paid membership
that unlocks free shipping, extended warranty, and priority support.

## Support hours
Our support team is available 7 days a week, 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM (local time).
Live chat and email are answered within a few minutes during these hours.

- Escalations / complaints: care@techmart.example

## Account
You can create a free account to track orders, save addresses, and manage your
Premium membership. Password resets are handled from the login page under
"Forgot password".

## Payments accepted
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, UPI, and TechMart gift cards.
All payments are processed securely; we never store full card numbers.
