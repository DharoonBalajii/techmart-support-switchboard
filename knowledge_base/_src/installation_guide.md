# TechMart Electronics — Setup & Troubleshooting Guide

## Activating TechMart Premium
1. Sign in at techmart.example and open **Account → Membership**.
2. If you just paid and Premium still shows "locked", **sign out and back in** to
   refresh your entitlements.
3. If it is still locked after 15 minutes, your payment may be confirmed but the
   entitlement failed to sync. Contact support with your order ID — we can
   activate it manually and, if we can't, issue a refund.

## Password reset
1. On the login page, click **Forgot password**.
2. Enter your account email; a reset link is valid for 30 minutes.
3. If the email doesn't arrive, check spam and confirm you're using the email on
   file. Still stuck? Contact support to verify your identity.

## Login problems
- "Invalid credentials": reset your password.
- "Account locked": too many attempts — wait 15 minutes or contact support.
- Two-factor code not arriving: check the time on your device and your signal.

## Hub Mini setup
1. Plug in the Hub Mini and wait for the ring light to pulse blue.
2. Open the TechMart Home app → **Add device** → follow the pairing prompts.
3. If pairing fails, hold the reset button for 10 seconds and retry.

## App won't update or crashes
- Update to the latest app version from your app store.
- Clear the app cache, restart the device, and try again.
- Persistent crashes: note any error code and send it to support.
